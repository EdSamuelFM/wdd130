White Water Rafting Adventures

Our mission is to provide safe, exciting, and memorable white water rafting experiences for adventurers of all skill levels. We're passionate about sharing the thrill of the river with everyone!

History

Founded in 1995 by river enthusiasts Mark and Sarah Johnson, White Water Rafting Adventures began as a small family operation with just two rafts. What started as a passion project quickly grew into one of the region's most respected rafting companies.

Over the years, we've introduced thousands of people to the thrill of white water rafting while maintaining our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and creating unforgettable experiences on the river.
